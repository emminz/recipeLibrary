#Sima
[Boil a few liters of the water and pour over the sugar. Add the rest of the water cold. Add yeast, mix well. Add sliced lemons. Let sit in room temperature for a day, bottle and move to the fridge. Add a dash of sugar and a few raisins to the bottom of every bottle. Sima is done when the raisins float to the top.
%80 dl § water ¤ 11 dl § sugar ¤ 0.25 tsp § yeast ¤ 2 § lemon
-----
#Butter bean & chorizo stew
[Slice the chorizo and tip into a large saucepan over a medium heat. Fry gently for 5 mins or until starting to turn dark brown. Add the tomatoes and butter beans, bring to the boil, then simmer for 10 mins. Swirl through the pesto, season lightly and ladle into four bowls.
%200 g § chorizo ¤ 800 g § chopped tomatoes ¤ 800 g § butter beans ¤ 1 tub § pesto
-----
